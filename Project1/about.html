About Us

Our History

Formed in the early 2000s, Catching Roses has become synonymous with captivating performances and enchanting covers of your favorite songs. Our dynamic lineup of talented musicians brings together harmonious vocals, skilled instrumentals, and a personalized touch that resonates with audiences of all ages.

From romantic ballads to energetic pop hits and timeless classics, our diverse repertoire spans a wide range of genres, ensuring there's something for everyone. We take pride in infusing each song with our unique style and energy, while staying true to the essence of the original, creating an unforgettable experience for you and your guests.

Our journey began in local venues, where we quickly gained a loyal following for our exceptional talent. Word of our captivating performances spread, and soon we were being sought after by couples looking for that extra touch of magic on their wedding day. Our reputation for creating unforgettable moments led us to perform at high-profile weddings, including celebrity and destination events.

Beyond our live performances, Catching Roses has also made waves online, with our videos going viral and attracting a significant following. We are humbled by the love and support we receive from our fans and the joy we bring to couples around the world.

With Catching Roses, your wedding celebration will be elevated to new heights. We pride ourselves on professionalism, versatility, and the ability to adapt to your unique musical preferences. Our goal is to make your special day an unforgettable experience for you and your guests, filled with love, joy, and cherished memories.
